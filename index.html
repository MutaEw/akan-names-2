NAMING OF CHILDREN IN GHANA

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian names corresponding to the day a child is born!.

MALE

  • Sunday : Kwasi
  • Monday : Kwadwo
  • Tuesday : Kwabena
  • Wednesday : Kwaku
  • Thursday : Yaw
  • Friday : Kofi
  • Saturday : Kwame

Names are very important. It tells a story of who we are, what we shall be and become. Therefore as parents when naming our children we should think very carefully before naming our children, how the name will impact on our children as they grow up, especially in this world of inequality.
